Privacy Policy // No Tracking. No Accounts. Just Air.

01. The EZ Philosophy

EZ Breathe was built to be the antidote to bloated wellness apps. We believe that breathing should be simple, private, and free from corporate surveillance. Our data policy is simple: We don't want your data, we don't track your habits, and we have no servers to store them on.

02. Data Collection & Usage

EZ Breathe is an offline-first utility. This means:

  • No Accounts: You never have to log in. Your sessions are yours alone.
  • No Analytics: We do not track how often you use the app or which breathing patterns you prefer.
  • Local Only: All settings and preferences are stored locally on your device hardware.

03. Permissions

  • Audio: Used only to play your selected ambient background loops.
  • Health Integration (Optional): If enabled, we write "Mindful Minutes" to your device's health database. We never read or export this data.
THE BOTTOM LINE: You bought the app, you own the experience. Your data never leaves your pocket.
